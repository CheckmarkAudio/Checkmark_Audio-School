Your Enrollment Journey

Overview: Your journey starts by letting us know you're interested. This form helps us get to know you and make sure Checkmark Audio School is the right fit for your goals.

  • Fill out your name, email, and a few questions about your experience and goals.
  • Select full tuition or payment plan.
  • Our team reviews your info and reaches out within 48 hours to schedule a consultation.

Overview: Meet with a member of our team to talk through the course, your goals, and how we can help.

  • Choose a time for an in-person or virtual meeting.
  • We walk you through curriculum details and answer questions.
  • If you’re a good fit, you’ll be invited to officially enroll.

Overview: Select your payment method—pay in full or finance through our in-house plan.

  • Review tuition total and any gear/software add-ons.
  • Choose full payment or in-house plan (10% down, 10% interest).
  • Submit 10% deposit to reserve your spot.

Overview: After payment, sign our official Enrollment Agreement to secure your spot and access onboarding materials.

  • Read the Student Enrollment Agreement (policies, refund terms, etc.).
  • Electronically sign or submit a paper copy.
  • Receive your syllabus, class schedule, and onboarding checklist.

In-House Payment Plan

  • Pay 20% down upfront to reserve your spot.
  • Finance the remaining 80% over your course duration.
  • A 10% interest rate applies to the financed amount.
  • Monthly installments begin on your program start date.

Other Loan Options You Can Explore

  • Federal PLUS Loans (for parents/graduates)—apply at studentaid.gov.
  • Private Education Loans via banks (Sallie Mae, Discover)—compare APRs and terms.
  • Personal Loans from credit unions or online lenders.
  • Credit Card Financing (0% APR promos)—watch for post-promo rates.
  • Scholarships & Grants—search local and national opportunities specific to audio programs.

You may pay off any financed balance early without penalty.
