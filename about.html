Our Mission & Vision

At Checkmark School of Audio, our goal is to establish Albuquerque’s first in-person audio engineering and music production school—combining pro studio experience with hands-on mentorship to launch the next generation of recording engineers.

Expert Instructors

Supportive Community

A collaborative environment where peers and mentors push you to excel.
