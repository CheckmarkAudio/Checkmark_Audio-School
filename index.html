About Our Curriculum

At Checkmark School of Audio, we’ve crafted a curriculum that mirrors exactly what happens in today’s top commercial studios. From your very first class, you’ll be rolling up your sleeves and working on real-world projects—no theory lectures, no busywork. You’ll learn by doing, guided every step of the way by instructors with decades of professional experience.

You have two powerful options: Choose any one of our 2-month modules to focus intensely on Production, Recording, or Mixing & Mastering—or go all-in with our Complete Course Package, where you’ll progress through all three modules over six months, plus receive bonus career coaching and 1:1 mentoring.

  • Hands-On Studio Training: Use the same consoles, mics, and outboard gear you’ll find in pro facilities.
  • Project-Based Learning: Build your own tracks, record live artists, and mix release-ready masters as you learn.
  • Industry Expertise: Get personalized feedback from veteran engineers and producers who’ve worked on major-label releases.
  • Flexible Pathways: Tailor your journey—dip into a single module or complete the full 6-month program for maximum depth.
