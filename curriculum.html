Complete Course Curriculum

Our 6-month Complete Course Package takes you from your first beat to a polished, release-ready track. You’ll move through three focused 2-month modules—Production, Recording, and Mixing/Mastering—while also gaining hands-on career coaching and one-on-one mentoring.

Module 1: Music Production & Creative Sound Design

Duration: Weeks 1–8 (16 sessions, twice weekly)

  1. Week 1: Hooks, Layers & Arrangement Planning
  2. Week 2: Drum Kit Creation & Sample Library Building
  3. Week 3: Plugins, Synthesis & Sound Design Basics
  4. Week 4: Drum Programming & Rhythm Theory
  5. Week 5: Harmony, Melody & Bass Writing
  6. Week 6: Arrangement Execution & Session Polish
  7. Week 7: Transitions, Effects & Automation
  8. Week 8: Final Project Export & Template Creation

Module 2: Studio Recording & Audio Engineering

Duration: Weeks 9–16 (16 sessions, twice weekly)

  1. Week 9: Signal Flow & Tube Preamp Shootout
  2. Week 10: Microphone Shootout & Polar Pattern Testing
  3. Week 11: Multi-Mic Setups & Acoustic Evaluation
  4. Week 12: Instrument Tracking & Hardware Integration
  5. Week 13: Vocal Tracking with Analog Chains (preamps, gates, EQ, compression)
  6. Week 14: Editing, Comping & Session Cleanup
  7. Week 15: FX & Automation for Tracked Audio
  8. Week 16: Studio Workflow, Troubleshooting & Review

Module 3: Mixing, Mastering & Delivery

Duration: Weeks 17–24 (16 sessions, twice weekly)

  1. Week 17: Critical Listening & Analyzing Pro Mixes
  2. Week 18: Session Setup, Basic Balance & Panning
  3. Week 19: Masking Solutions with EQ & Compression
  4. Week 20: Time-Based Effects & Creative Automation
  5. Week 21: Mix Planning Using Reference Tracks
  6. Week 22: Full Personal Song Mix (Top-Down Method)
  7. Week 23: Mastering: Loudness, EQ & Stereo Imaging
  8. Week 24: Final Review, Delivery & Class Presentation
